[[Sepultura]] (seh‐pull‐TUR‐uh) is an enormous, fortified necropolis that stands at the center of [[Syndramas]]. Built from gravestone and [[drakkonite]], this mighty capital serves as a neutral territory where all [[The Sepulchral Legions|three legions]] have agreed to a truce while within the city's walls and for ten miles beyond its limits. Violating this non-aggression pact is considered such an egregious and dishonorable taboo that even the most treacherous and power-hungry people of the province find such a thing unthinkable. The rare schemer or brute who engages in any act of war within the city is personally dealt with by [[Wyrm Queen Sapphira]] herself within her mighty [[drakkonite]] citadel at the heart of the necropolis. 

[[Sepultura]] is carved up into quarters—the [[Frost Quarter]], the [[Blood Quarter]], and the [[Bone Quarter]] for the [[The Rimeshade Legion|Rimeshade]], [[The Bloodboil Legion|Bloodboil]], and [[The Marrowfeast Legion|Marrowfeast]] Legions, respectively, and the [[Wyrm Quarter]], which acts as neutral ground for meeting and mingling amongst members of opposing factions and for conducting military business with [[Wyrm Queen Sapphira|Sapphira]]'s personal army, the [[Queensguard]]. While visitors from other provinces are rare, much coin can be made in offering goods and services of comfort to soldiers who experience so few of life's pleasures.
