While the entire province of [[Picco]] is one big circus, the capital of [[Funtopia]] (fun‐TOH‐pee‐uh) is the heart of the festivities. Equal parts carnival, amusement park, and city, it is a wonder of magical engineering that allows for a society of living toys to thrive. A night’s work here usually consists of playing games for hours to earn [[Fun Tickets]]. [[Funtopia]]’s food is rarely nutritious but supremely delicious, drawing adventurous eaters from across [[Druskenvald]] to sample the endless creative options, especially the legendary [[Jolly Jangles Waffles]]. The transportation system consists of elaborately constructed rides that carry commuters across town at extreme speeds, filling the air with screams of thrill and delight as they are delivered to any of the city’s themed districts.
