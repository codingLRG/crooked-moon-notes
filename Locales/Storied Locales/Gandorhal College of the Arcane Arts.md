Located on [[Lake Rommeda]] (ROM‐meh‐duh) in the misty moors of [[Astramar]], [[Gandorhal College of the Arcane Arts|Gandorhal]] (GAN‐der‐hall) is [[Druskenvald]]’s most prestigious magical academy. Students from across all thirteen provinces are drawn to this influential college to hone their arcane skills and knowledge by following a general curriculum that covers all types of magic.

However, the institution provides the opportunity for students to specialize in one particular school, dividing each year’s class into eight houses to foster the growth of talent in each pupil’s selected focus. The labyrinthine halls of [[Gandorhal College of the Arcane Arts|Gandorhal]] are filled with mysteries and dangers that some consider unacceptable for students so young, but the professors believe that the necessity to defend oneself with magic is the best path to mastery.
