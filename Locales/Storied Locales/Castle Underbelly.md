The capital city of [[Bubonia]] is built into the ruins of a colossal castle that had long been abandoned before recorded history. A strange sight to newcomers, this sprawling city is one of the largest population centers in [[Druskenvald]] and features ramshackle wooden additions grafted to ancient stone structures.

The city is home to thousands of people of all levels of prosperity, separated by the different layers of the castle, with the rich and powerful residing at the tops of great towers connected by heavily guarded walkways, and the most downtrodden attempting to survive far below in a massive sewer system. It also contains the headquarters of all five of the great guilds, each prospering from the surprising amount of trade, commerce, and travel throughout the capital.
