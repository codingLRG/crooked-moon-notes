The most sacred site in [[Syndrama]]s is a vast dragon graveyard in a hidden valley in the northernmost mountain range of the province. At [[Dragons' End|Dragon's End]], miles of snowy landscape are covered in the massive skeletal remains of thousands of wyrms, drakes, and other dragonkin. The valley was the site of the bloodiest battle of the civil war that rendered the winter dragons extinct, and [[Wyrm Queen Sapphira|Sapphira]] occasionally makes a pilgrimage here to reflect in solitude—the only time she allows herself to feel any grief.

Legend states that many powerful artifacts of war were lost in the battle and still wait, scattered among the bones. But would-be grave robbers must face numerous dangers, including the unforgiving elements, huge scavenging beasts that still feed on the dragons' marrow, and lingering curses that helped bring an end to a civilization.
