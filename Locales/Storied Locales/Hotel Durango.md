Despite what some folks say, [[Hotel Durango]] (der‐AYNG‐goh) isn’t a myth but it sure is hard to find. When someone’s luck runs out in [[Chernabos]] and they lose everything, they often wander off into the desert in delirious damnation. It’s to these lost souls that [[Hotel Durango]] appears. An impossibly large and remote hotel in the middle of the desert, it acts as a shimmering oasis for the desperate. The lights are inviting, and the door is always open, encouraging those who see it to stop for the night and forget their troubles.

Within the mirage-like hotel is everything one’s heart might desire: water for the thirsty, food for the starving, vices for the craving, companionship for the lonely, or some chips for the gambler who wants one last game. Once a soul checks into Hotel Durango, they leave the world they knew behind, find everything they’ll ever need, and can never leave. However, certain tales whisper of ways to find the hotel and free the souls within.
