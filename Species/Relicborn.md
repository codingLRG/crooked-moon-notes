[[Relicborn]] are a skeleton-like species native to [[Kalero]], generally emerging from the numerous tombs and crypts that line the canyon walls of the province. They thrive in a culture of celebration and remembrance, which infuses the very essence of every member of the species. Dancing between the energy of a vivacious party and the serenity of peaceful reflection, this folk holds a deep appreciation for both the living and the dead.

They appear as skeletons decorated with a wide array of vibrant colors, patterns, and styles, and their bones are encased in a translucent, glowing substance that provides them with a similar mass and shape to other Humanoid species, allowing them to comfortably wear clothing and armor. Some [[Relicborn]] can even grow hair from their skulls, which is often shaped into bombastic styles. [[Relicborn]] typically live for 250 years before losing their form and collapsing into a pile of bones
