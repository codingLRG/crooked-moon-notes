[[Stoneborn]] are a gargoyle-like species native to [[Pholsense]], usually meticulously carved by the lord of the province, [[Archbishop Danton Alexandre Renault]]. Blessed by the divine power known as the [[The Silver Path|Silver Path]], these people can call upon the godliness that suffuses their construction. In a domain of darkness, they have looked to the light of their god to build a civilization that attempts to uphold what is good and lawful at all costs.

Their stony skin matches the same hue and pattern as the stone they were carved from. While all of their kind have broad wings that carry their dense, heavy bodies, their biology can evoke a wide variety of creatures—including bats, fiends, dragons, birds, angels, dogs, and lizards— often combining features into a chimeric blend. They usually live for 300 years before crumbling into rubble.
