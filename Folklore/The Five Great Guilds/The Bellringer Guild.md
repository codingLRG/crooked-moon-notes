Often hidden away in crumbling abbeys across [[Bubonia]], this order of monks, priests, and prophets worships the pestilent haze as a deity called the [[Great Plagued One]]. As the keepers of the primary faith of the province, they command legions of zealous adherents, spreading the holy word while administering salves and ointments that cure and spread the plague, all in the name of their pestilent god.
