In [[Picco]], it's not gold that buys goods and services. Instead, [[Fun Tickets]] are the province's currency, which take the form of shiny carnival tickets in vibrant colors that represent different uses, from purchasing food to securing passage in one of the huge hot air balloons that soar across the province. These tickets drive [[Funtopia]]’s economy—which by all reasonable accounts should simply not function—and they can be obtained in a variety of ways, including purchasing with coin, earning through work, winning as a game prize, or rewarded for succeeding in an incredible challenge.

Rare and uniquely designed tickets procured through particularly death-defying feats have specialized magical power. Avid collectors travel from across [[Druskenvald]] in the hopes of finding these rare and prestigious prizes. There are rumors of exclusive attractions and artifacts that require one of these precious tickets to gain access.
