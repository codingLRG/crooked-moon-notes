[[The Silver Path]] is not merely a religion or divine philosophy; it is the god itself. Lacking the typical trappings of personification that mortals often attribute to their gods, this deity exists as an imperceivable divine will that upholds the five holy tenets: piousness, temperance, diligence, unity, and sacrifice. There is zero tolerance for sin or apathy that affronts these virtues. [[Pholsense]] has suffered far worse from the witchcraft of the [[Coven of the Midnight Moon]] than any other province, which has only emboldened their righteous fury.

Most of [[Pholsense]], including many of the [[Stoneborn]] and other species of the province, serve as devout members of the faith, celebrating with regular ceremonies in a variety of houses of worship, from small chapels to grand cathedrals. All throughout the region, the near-constant ringing of great bells can be heard; the faithful claim this is the voice of the [[The Silver Path|Silver Path]] and attempt to discern meaning from it. However, members of the hierarchical clergy insist only they can truly decipher the voice of their god, so they act as intermediaries with increasing authority all the way to the head of the church, [[Archbishop Danton Alexandre Renault|Archbishop Renault]].
