In the great conflict that destroyed the ruined kingdom that once thrived in [[Syndramas]], countless mighty dragons perished, and their broken bodies have been covered by the frozen wastes. As mass death fell upon the great creatures by the thousands, their draconic essence was leeched into the province itself, forever changing the land and creating a new metal ore that would be called [[drakkonite]] (DRACK‐oh‐nite).

Vast deposits of this dark and unnaturally cold metal can be found throughout [[Syndramas]]. Known for its immense sharpness, strength, and lightness, it is widely used across the province in the construction of weapons, armor, and structures. Because it is so highly sought after, even by warriors outside [[Syndramas]], many of the deadliest battles have been fought over [[drakkonite]] mines.
