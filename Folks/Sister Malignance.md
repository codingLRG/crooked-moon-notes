Gaunt and shrouded in a billowing black robe, the nearly skeletal [[Sister Malignance]] lingers atop [[Pale Mountain]], which stands at the southernmost border of [[Chernabos]]. As the eldest of [[Mother Midnight]]’s daughters, the occult power of [[Sister Malignance|Malignance]] is immense, and she has used her mastery of necromancy to command death itself. 

Every night, she rides her white Undead steed along the towering wall of shrieking fog at [[Druskenvald]]’s border and pulls forth twisted, tormented souls that she dominates to her will. Whether bid by her mother or motivated by her own aims, [[Sister Malignance]] works tirelessly to weaken the Shroud’s barrier so that, like a breaking dam, a tide of death will spill forth and crash upon [[Chernabos]] and beyond.
