Born into a family of adventures, [[Relic the Artificier]], [[Hugthara the Barbarian]], [[Dynasta the Rogue]]. [[Hugthara the Barbarian]] went into labor while in the middle of an adventure, came out small but expecting.

Sue wasn't fond of dungeon delving, as her overall clumsy and not stealthy, made the prospects of becoming a on the ground adventurer unrealistic, however found passion in medicine, specifically practical. [[Dulic the Artificier]] pushed for another spellcaster in the family, and had Sue specialize in Necromancy for healing magic. Oftentimes pushing back on expeditions, whenever she arrived, she would often times find herself unconscious mid way through, and found herself on the [[Ghostlight Express]].

On her final mission, after exploring the crypt of a aspiring cult leader with the secrets of being lich, a stealth mission turned loud after a trip exposed Sue to the undead army. Faced with an undead ogre, Sue nearly presented to being a new passenger on the [[Ghostlight Express]], [[Dynasta the Rogue]] unveiled from the shadows to slay the beast. Losing too many hitpoints at once, [[Dynasta the Rogue]] became paralyzed from the waist down and the family retreated from the crypt.

Sue not being skilled enough to repair the injury in the dungeon, [[Dynasta the Rogue]]'s condition became unfixable by the time a skilled cleric could assist.

[[Dynasta the Rogue]] would later fall deathly ill due to medical complications.

The Dracoglask family would cease all adventuring due to a lack of motivation with their late daughter's death. Sue blaming herself believed the only way to return their family to their former glory was to cast an enhanced revivify spell that would cure from her disabled state.

Delving into grand necromancy tombs without a proper understanding, attempting to revive her fallen sister has accidentally caused the spell to go arrie and lose all aspects of herself.
