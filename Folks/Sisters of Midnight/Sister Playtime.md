As the youngest, smallest, and most frail of [[Mother Midnight]]'s daughters, [[Sister Playtime]] is consumed by envy—of her sisters’ power and station, of the joyous community shared among [[Kalero]]'s people, and of the toys, friends, and parties she never had. Appearing like an uncanny doll with a porcelain face, Playtime has mastered the magic of memory and has the mind to turn her victims into her own living playthings.

Not caring to earn true companions, she wipes and manipulates the minds and memories of the province's people so they will offer her the adoration she desperately craves. She makes them play games with her that often end in disaster and death. Every night, her jealousy builds, and she will not be satisfied until all of [[Kalero]] is her personal playground.
