Said to be the only [[Silkborn]] that has ascended to divinity and achieved immortality, [[God King Xanatep]] (ZAN‐uh‐tep) is the supreme ruler of [[Nerukhet]]. He begrudgingly pledges fealty to [[Phillip Druskenvald]] under the condition that he can rule his people as he sees fit. As the master of [[Vekloriash the Amethyst Dread]]—the largest and deadliest bejeweled scorpion to ever exist and the only to possess great dragonfly wings—he has remained unopposed since before the doom of the First Kingdom. While his pride is unmatched, his true aim is to increase the greatness of [[Nerukhet]] above all else.

Ruling from the top of the colossal monument-flanked ziggurat in [[Arakir, the Skittering City]], [[God King Xanatep|Xanatep]] allows the various factions, houses, and families to squabble and settle disputes on their own, even as it comes to war. However, if such a conflict ever threatens the security or prosperity of the province, he descends upon the battle atop [[Vekloriash the Amethyst Dread]] and brings an end to the fighting, often resulting in the total annihilation of both sides. Through the [[Tears of the Gods]], he can see across all of [[Nerukhet]], so few escape his watchful and merciless gaze.
