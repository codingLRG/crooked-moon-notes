[[Catrina the Sweet]] (kuh‐TREE‐nuh) leads the province like she leads the dance during [[Kalero]]'s unending celebrations—with unbound vivaciousness and a lust for life that can only be truly appreciated by the dead. As a [[Relicborn]], her skeletal features are adorned with a rainbow of intricate patterns and draped in swirling silks, jangling jewelry, sweet perfumes, and a veritable mane of curly locks that seem to have a mind of their own.

While her unbound enthusiasm, joy, and passion for dancing and pageantry lead some adversaries to dismiss her as a legitimate threat, such folly always leads to defeat or death. Hand-in-hand with [[Catrina the Sweet|Catrina]]’s gregarious delight is a playful cunning and measured ferocity that she leverages to fiercely protect the past, present, and future of her province. Embracing the beauty of death has made Catrina one of the most respected and even feared leaders among the nobility of the [[Crescent Court]].
