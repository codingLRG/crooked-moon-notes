As well as ruling the whole of [[Astramar]], [[Headmistress Narcissa Voss|Narcissa Voss]] (nar‐SISS‐uh VAHSS) serves as the headmistress of the illustrious magical academy [[Gandorhal College of the Arcane Arts|Gandorhal]]. As an [[Azureborn]], her features are owl-like, with white feathers that almost appear as spectacles that frame wizened yet determined blue eyes. [[Headmistress Narcissa Voss|Voss]] is known among the other nobles of the [[Crescent Court ]]for being especially stern, proud, and cold, but she exhibits surprising warmth and patience with all of her students and prioritizes their tutelage above most of her other duties.

When she is not overseeing the academy or ruling the province from her tower in [[Lunarium]], teleporting between both locations with a single incantation, she looks to the stars as a master of divination, seeking to foresee threats to her people. While she can see many possible futures, fate can always be twisted and changed, and she often joins her most powerful mages in weaving monumental spells that protect the peoples of [[Astramar]].
