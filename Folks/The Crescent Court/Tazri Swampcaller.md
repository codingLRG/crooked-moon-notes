Although the [[Bogborn]] spirit priest called [[Tazri Swampcaller]] (TAZ‐ree) is a member of the [[Crescent Court]], she eschews any political authority and leads [[Zulrogg]] by example and spiritual guidance. Personally chosen by the great [[Hydra Spirit]] to be its speaker, she receives many heads' worth of wisdom and is widely considered to be one of the most insightful beings in [[Druskenvald]]. Despite her station, she spends much of her nights teaching the youth how to better commune with the spirits and discover which [[Spirit Beasts|Spirit Beast]] they should choose to favor—a profound rite of passage for every member of [[Zulrogg]] society.

[[Tazri Swampcaller|Tazri]] has lived for many centuries, extending her life with [[Everblooms|Everbloom]] concoctions until she can find a suitable replacement to become the next speaker of the [[Hydra Spirit]]. However, when one looks upon her, it is difficult to determine her age, appearing youthful one moment and truly ancient the next. This is made more mystifying by her eccentric passion for collecting. She knows that the swamp never gives without purpose, so she takes any trinket or debris she finds and adds it to the stitched sack that holds her collection. It is said that these odds and ends become the most powerful reagents in her rituals.
